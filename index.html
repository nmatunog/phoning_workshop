Master Your Time, Master Your Success

Welcome! This workshop is designed to help you master the critical skills of calendar management and effective phoning. Time is your most valuable asset as an advisor. By learning to manage it with intentionality, you can unlock your full potential.

This module is based on the principles from "Bridge Your Gap" by Jim Effner.

Activity: Calculate Your "Billable Hours"

Discover how much of your day is truly productive. The goal is to have at least 60% of your day consist of "billable hours."

The 8 Steps to Mastery

Step 1: Create Your Ideal Calendar

Master your calendar with an abundance mentality. Your goal isn't just to be busy, but to see the right people. Fill your calendar with intentionality and purpose.

"Every time you say 'yes' to something, you are saying 'no' to something else."

Step 2: Relentlessly Fill Appointment Slots

View your calendar like a hotel businessâ€”every empty room (or time slot) is a lost opportunity. Aim for at least 60% of your day to be "billable hours."

Track your business, strategize, and work with intention.

Step 3: Don't Compromise Your Plan

The hardest part of calendar management is living it. Define your "sacred times" for phoning, planning, or personal life, and protect them fiercely.

Do not compromise your planned slots to meet the needs of one client.

Step 4: Honor Your Phoning Time

Treat your scheduled phoning time as if it were an appointment with your most important client. Don't cancel it. This builds discipline and reinforces its importance.

Consistent phoning leads to an ideal calendar.

Step 5: Use Identical Phoning Language

Design a script that is authentic, effective, and laser-focused on getting the appointment. Practice it until it sounds natural, not like a telemarketer.

Always end by offering two specific time slots that need to be filled.

Step 6: Memorize Objection Responses

You will encounter the same few objections repeatedly. Prepare and memorize your responses so that they never throw you off your game. Confidence comes from preparation.

Step 7: Go Through Three Objections

People are often programmed to say "no" the first time. A successful advisor doesn't give up easily. Aim to work through three objections before suggesting to reconnect in 6 months.

Step 8: Stand Up While Phoning

Your energy is contagious. Standing up improves your volume, tone, and inflection. Let your passion and smile jump through the phone and grab the prospect's attention.
